# Product Hunt Launch

**Category:** Marketing
**Priority:** P2 Medium
**Effort:** M
**Status:** Not Started

## Summary

Prepare and execute a Product Hunt launch to gain visibility in the developer and tech community. Product Hunt is one of the most effective channels for launching developer tools and Mac utilities.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Product Hunt maker account created
- [ ] Product page prepared with all assets
- [ ] Launch date selected (Tuesday-Thursday)
- [ ] First comment drafted
- [ ] Support network notified before launch
- [ ] Post monitored and engaged for 24 hours
- [ ] Follow-up thank you post after launch

## Technical Notes

### Pre-Launch Preparation

#### 1. Create Maker Account
- Sign up at [producthunt.com](https://www.producthunt.com)
- Complete profile with photo and bio
- Become active in community (upvote, comment) for at least 1 week before launch

#### 2. Prepare Assets

**Thumbnail (240x240)**
- App icon or logo
- Clear at small size

**Gallery Images (1270x760)**
1. Hero shot - menu bar expanded showing all features
2. Local ports view - framework detection in action
3. Website monitoring - status and response times
4. Docker containers - container management
5. Menu bar states - green/orange/red indicators

**GIF/Video (optional)**
- 30-second demo showing key workflow
- Screen recording with annotations

### Launch Day Strategy

#### Timing
- **Best days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Launch time:** 12:01 AM PST (to maximize 24-hour window)
- **Avoid:** Weekends, major holidays, Apple events

#### Engagement Plan

**Hour 0-2:** Respond to every comment immediately
**Hour 2-8:** Check every 30 minutes
**Hour 8-16:** Check every hour
**Hour 16-24:** Check every 2 hours

### Support Network

Before launch, reach out to:
- Friends/colleagues who use Product Hunt
- Developer communities (Discord, Slack)
- Twitter followers
- Email list (if any)

Ask them to:
1. Upvote early in the day
2. Leave a genuine comment
3. Share on their social media

**Don't ask for fake engagement**‚Äîit's against PH rules and obvious.

### Post-Launch

#### Day 1-2
- Thank everyone who supported
- Respond to remaining comments
- Share results on social media

#### Day 3-7
- Write a retrospective (what worked, what didn't)
- Follow up with people who showed interest
- Implement quick-win feature suggestions
